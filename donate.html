Monetary Donations

Monetary donations are always welcome and greatly appreciated. They can be made via Paypal using all major credit cards.

Monetary donations can also be mailed to: Friends of the Plymouth Pound P.O. Box 578 Manomet, MA 02345 Please do not mail cash. Checks can also be dropped off at the Kingston Collection Mall during open hours.

Supply Donations

Donations of pet food and pet supplies are a great donation alternative and can be made to the Pet Food Bank located at our adoption center at the Kingston Collection Mall during open hours. Wish List: Friskies wet canned cat food, classic pate Friskies dry cat food Temptations cat treats - any flavor Fresh Step non-clumping clay cat litter Dawn dish soap Paper towels Cat toys Soft cat blankets Roccal disinfectant Lysol disinfectant spray Cat beds Leashes, harnesses and collars for large-sized dogs Gift cards for Stop & Shop, Target, Wal-Mart or Petco

Amazon Smile

Shop using Amazon Smile. When shopping on Amazon.com, use smile.amazon.com instead, and select Friends of the Plymouth Pound as your charity. A portion of all of your Amazon purchases will be donated to Friends of the Plymouth Pound!
